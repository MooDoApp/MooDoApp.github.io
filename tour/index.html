FAQ
How much does Moo.do cost?

The Moo.do website and apps are free! We may add additional paid features and products in the future.

Why do I need to login with my Google account?

Moo.do uses Google Drive to store your documents which provides a secure method of storage and high availability of your data.

Does Moo.do read my personal data or scan the rest of my Google Drive?

No. At no point does Moo.do access documents that it did not create. Additionally no data you enter into Moo.do is sent back to our servers. See our privacy policy for more information.

Is Moo.do the organizational app for me?

Absolutely! Moo.do provides tools for anyone to keep their lives in order. It supports a broad range of organizational styles and is flexible enough to be used in a wide variety of ways.

Should I have an issue, how do I get support?

Send us an email to support@moo.do and we will get back to you quickly with a solution or contact us on twitter @MooDoApp. We are fully committed to quickly resolving any issues you may have and we'd love your feedback and ideas on how we can improve your experience.

Why is Moo.do right for my personal organization? Small businesses?

Moo.do was designed from the ground up to excel at managing all aspects of your personal life whether its making sure you never lose your grocery list or planning your next vacation to an exotic destination.

Because of this flexibility, Moo.do also works extremely well at managing projects and making sure nothing falls through the cracks. We've been using Moo.do to plan and organize our work on Moo.do since day one.
