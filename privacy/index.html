Information We Collect

We collect "Non-Personal Information" and "Personal Information". Non-Personal Information includes information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data, general demographic information we may collect, referring/exit pages and URLs, platform types, preferences you submit and preferences that are generated based on the data you submit and number of clicks. Personal Information includes only your email, which you submit to us through the registration process at the site. We do not share any Personal Information with any third parties.

Google Drive

None of the information entered into your document is sent to us or stored on our servers. All information is saved on Google Drive and subject to the Google Privacy Policy. Moo.do will only access and modify files that it has created on your Google Drive account. Moo.do does not have access to your other files.

Authorization Scopes

By default, Moo.do will request access to the UserInfo.Email, Drive.File, Drive.Install, and Drive.AppData OAuth scopes. These allow Moo.do to access only files that were created with Moo.do and allow Moo.do to see your Google email address. Over the course of using Moo.do you may need to grant additional authorization scopes. These include the Contacts.Readonly and Tasks.Readonly scopes. Any data that is accessed through these scopes is only sent to devices that you use Moo.do with. Moo.do does not send or store any of this information anywhere except locally on your devices.
