Privacy Policy
Information We Collect
We collect "Non-Personal Information" and "Personal Information". Non-Personal Information includes information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data, general demographic information we may collect, referring/exit pages and URLs, platform types, preferences you submit and preferences that are generated based on the data you submit and number of clicks. Personal Information includes only your email, which you submit to us through the registration process at the site. We do not share any Personal Information with 3rd parties.
Google Drive
None of the information entered into your document is sent to us or stored on our servers. All information is saved on Google Drive and subject to the Google Privacy Policy. Moo.do will only access and modify files that it has created on your Drive.
Authorization Scopes
By default, Moo.do will request access to the UserInfo.Email, Drive.File, Drive.Install, and Drive.AppData OAuth scopes. These allow Moo.do access files that were created with Moo.do and allow Moo.do to see your Google email address. Over the course of using Moo.do you may need to grant additional authorization scopes. These include the Contacts.Readonly and Tasks.Readonly scopes. Any data that is accessed through these scopes is only sent to devices that you connect to Moo.do with. Specifically information stored in files Moo.do did not create and any contact information for other people is not sent or stored anywhere except your device.

We will notify you of significant changes to our Privacy Policy by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site. Significant changes will go into effect 30 days following such notification. Non-material changes or clarifications will take effect immediately. You should periodically check the Site and this privacy page for updates.
