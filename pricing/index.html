Cancellation and Refund Policy
We currently do not provide refunds for purchased services. You may cancel your subscription at any time and we will stop billing you immediately. For monthly subscriptions this means we will not bill you the following month. For yearly subscriptions this means we will not bill you for the following year. Please contact support@moo.do if you mistakenly cancelled your subscription, would like to change your billing plan, or for further information.
