About Us
At Moo.do we believe in making powerful software as easy to use as possible. We created Moo.do because we got frustrated with the difficulty of staying organized and prioritizing multiple personal projects alongside work and our day-to-day lives. Our goal is to help you take control of your life without burdening you with complicated software.
The Team
Jay Meistrich
CO-FOUNDER - Seattle, WA
Prior to Moo.do, Jay worked in Microsoft's Applied Sciences Group where he helped start the Surface tablet project and researched future experiences for Windows, Windows Phone, Xbox, and Surface. Jay received his B.S. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University while exploring his passion for game development in the Game Creation Society. Jay is as a nomad, traveling and pursuing his loves of food and photography in his free time.
