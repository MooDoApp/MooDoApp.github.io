Change Log

June 21

  • Images in Google Drive can now be shown inline. Right click on a Drive file (that links to an image) and select “Show as image” to show it inline.
  • Dropping on a tag or contact block only replaces tag/contact if dropped from a corresponding block
  • Renamed “Delete Event” in item menu to “Remove from Google Calendar”

June 20

  • Allow drag/dropping of Drive Folders
  • Fixed autocomplete sometimes overwriting emails/files
  • Fixed settings not being scrollable on small screens
  • Fixed: Errors when creating items in Calendar view synced to Google Calendar
  • Fixed: The “Delete” menu option on calendar items also deletes from Google Calendar

June 17

  • Android: Fixed: Drag drop was sometimes not working right

  • Android: Fixed: Dragging emails to drop bucket

June 14

  • Insert link menu is better at removing links: a button to remove the link, and can clear link or text
