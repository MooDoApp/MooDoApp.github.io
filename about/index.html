Incredibly easy
Moo.do's content focused interface lets you organize in a way that makes sense to you. Moo.do does what you want, instead of the other way around.
Rather than fight a complex user interface, you can just write a @date to automatically add to your calendar. A +name lets you instantly start a call or email and #tags make it easy to search for similar items. Organize your life instead of organizing your organization system.
All in one
Moo.do organizes your projects, todo lists, calendar, contacts, reminders, events, and anything you can write down. You don't need to deal with multiple incompatible apps.
Todo List
Mark items as starred, high, medium, or low priority, and complete when done.
Planner
Schedule items by typing @ and a date. Your agenda tracks dates, priority, and starred items.
Outliner
Create projects and lists as deep as you want. Just press Tab to indent. Use #tags to add another dimension.
Notepad
Write everything down so you'll never forget anything.
Social
Everything is synchronized in realtime. Collaborate with your friends, family, and co-workers. Include +contacts to easily call or email them.
Focused
Concentrate on one project at a time and search to see just what you need. Open many views with independent searches to help you multitask.
