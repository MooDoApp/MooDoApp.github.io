Moo.do syncs with all of your devices and collaborators immediately as you type so you don't have to worry about saving or whether your other devices are up to date.
Collaboration
Plan a trip with a friend, keep a grocery list with your housemates, or manage a large project with your team. Share individual documents with different people while keeping your personal information private.
Contact Integration
Import your contacts and quickly access their phone number or email address on the go. Call them without leaving the app.
Offline Access
Your documents are stored on your devices so you can work with them even if you do not have an internet connection. Your data will sync as soon as you come online. Never worry about being left without access.
Private and Secure
You can feel comfortable knowing that your data is safe and secure. Moo.do uses Google Drive to store and synchronize your data. Moo.do only has access to files it creates and never sends your data to any other server. You can read more about our privacy policy here.
It's Your Data
We believe in you keeping ownership of your data. If your data is stuck in another application Moo.do provides tools to import it directly into the app. You may also export your data from Moo.do at any time in a variety of formats. Own your data.
